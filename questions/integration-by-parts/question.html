Integration by parts is an integration technique to try when the integrand is a product of two functions and can't be integrated by simpler methods, like $u$-substitution. The formula for integration by parts is written below: $$\int u dv = uv - \int v du$$

We choose part of the integrand to be $u$ and the rest to be $dv$. If we choose well, the integral becomes solvable. But be careful! For some choices of $u$ and $dv$, integration by parts can make things worse!
